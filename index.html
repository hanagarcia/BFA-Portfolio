"Bio"

Hana Garcia is an artist hailing from Brawley, California in the Imperial Valley. Th Hana draws infleunce to create from expereinces with others to create work that communicate the feelings and the experiences of others. She also draws influence from her experiences and feelings as a Mexican-American woman and her culture. Hana is a multi-medium artist who has experience working in HTML coding, creating virtual art galleries, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Premeire, and photography. Hana is currently pursuing a bachelor's in fine arts degree in digital media art at San Jose State University. Her artwork has been shown at the California Midwinter Fair, and at art gallery for gender and body positivity hosted by SJSU Gender Equity Center. Her work has been used by the San Jose State University Spartan Marching Band and by the Phi class of the Lambda Psi Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi at San Jose State University.
